VA Health Research Supporting Massage Therapy

The VA Health Research Center has conducted extensive studies on massage therapy‚Äôs role in reducing opioid prescriptions for chronic pain. Studies show massage therapy for veterans experiencing chronic pain can lead to decreased pain levels, increased mobility, and a reduction in opioid use among veterans. In fact, veterans engaged in the VA's Whole Health approach fostered by the VA-CCN benefit from complementary therapies like massage. Veterans experienced a 38% reduction in opioid use, compared to an 11% decrease among those not utilizing Whole Health services.
